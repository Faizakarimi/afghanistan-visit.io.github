Top three attractions in Afghanistan

Band-e-Amir National Park

It is one of the most beautiful places you can visit. Imagine, a beautifl lake inside a whole range of dry hills. The blue colour of the lake is just mesmerizing and deep. it is located in Bamyan City.

DarulAman Palace

Darul Aman Palace is a three-story-tall palace located in Darulaman, about 16 km south-west of the center of Kabul, Afghanistan. Surrounding the palace are the following buildings: the National Assembly, the National Museum of Afghanistan and the Afghan International University.

Herat Citadel

Great monument! Beautiful ancient architect and building, one the thousand beautiful historic places of Afghanistan.
